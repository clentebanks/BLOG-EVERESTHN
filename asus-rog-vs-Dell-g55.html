Gaming Laptop Showdown: ASUS ROG Strix Scar 18 vs. Dell G15 5535 (2025)

Looking for the ultimate gaming laptop in 2025? We compare two powerhouses that cater to very different needs: the beastly ASUS ROG Strix Scar 18 and the budget-friendly Dell G15 5535. Let’s break it down and help you decide.

Quick Comparison

Feature ASUS ROG Strix Scar 18 Dell G15 5535
CPUIntel Core Ultra 9 275HXAMD Ryzen 7 7840HS
GPURTX 5090 16GBRTX 4060 8GB
Display18" QHD+ 240Hz Mini-LED15.6" FHD 165Hz
RAM / Storage32–128GB / 2TB SSD16GB / 1TB SSD
Battery Life~6 hours~7 hours
Weight6.6 lbs5.2 lbs
Price$7,678$2,099

Dell G15 5535: Best Value for Gamers

The Dell G15 5535 delivers strong performance on a budget. It packs an RTX 4060 and Ryzen 7 combo that handles most games at high settings, perfect for students and casual gamers.

  • Pros: Affordable, solid specs, reliable thermals, decent battery.
  • Cons: Mid-range GPU, less premium feel.

Verdict: Best pick for everyday gamers who want solid performance without breaking the bank.

ASUS ROG Strix Scar 18: Power Unleashed

The Strix Scar 18 is a no-compromise machine. With a top-of-the-line Intel Core Ultra 9 CPU and RTX 5090 GPU, it demolishes every modern AAA title. The 240Hz Mini-LED panel makes gameplay buttery smooth and vibrant.

  • Pros: Unmatched performance, brilliant display, future-proof specs.
  • Cons: Expensive, heavy, moderate battery life.

Verdict: Ideal for pro gamers, creators, and competitive streamers.

Which One Should You Choose?

Both laptops are great, but it depends on your budget and gaming needs:

  • Choose ASUS ROG if you want maximum power, 4K gaming, and future-proof performance.
  • Choose Dell G15 if you want solid 1080p gaming on a budget with better portability.
